There are several National Security Agencies (most of them competing for investigative control but all in all attempting to prevent NATO from asserting control over their investigations into counter-insurgency).

-NATO Office of Naval Intelligence
-Interpol
-European Central Intelligence and National Security Agency (EuroInt)
-United States Central Intelligence Agency (CIA)
-United States Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI)
-Japanese Office of National Security (JNS)
-Chinese Unified Intelligence Office (C.Int)
-Nigerian Civilian Intelligence Agency (NCIA)

There are other offices from other countries cooperating as well, but their influence is mostly inconsequential compared to the ones listed above.
